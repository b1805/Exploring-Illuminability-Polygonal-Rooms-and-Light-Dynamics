Exploring Illuminability: Polygonal Rooms and Light Dynamics

This website contains the programs I have made for Prof. George Tokarsky as a Summer Undergraduate Research Assistant at the University of Alberta in 2024. The project website can be found here.

Programs:

  • Custom Star Room Illumination (Star Customizer)
  • Custom Un-illuminable Room Finder (Triangle Clicking)
  • Double Star
  • Custom Room Illuminator (Snakes & Bushes)

Videos:

I have recorded some videos and put them up on George Tokarsky's Youtube:

  • Trapped: An unilluminable room
  • The Double Myriad: A Tokarsky unilluminable room with 84 sides
  • The Double Star a 20 sided unilluminable room
  • Exploding Stars: An unilluminable end
  • The Strange Unilluminable Star
